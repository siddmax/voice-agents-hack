# Seat map stalls or selected seats do not load

## Customer Steps
1. Wait a few seconds for seat inventory to refresh.
2. Try a nearby section if the selected seat stays unavailable.
3. Restart the ticket selection flow if the spinner does not clear.

## Team Action
Expire stale inventory holds and show a clear unavailable-seat recovery path.

## Engineering Signal
Likely stale inventory cache, seat hold conflict, or delayed seat-map refresh.
