# Fees feel confusing or unexpectedly high at checkout

## Customer Steps
1. Open the order summary before payment.
2. Compare listed fees against the ticket subtotal and delivery option.
3. Contact support if the total changes after seat selection.

## Team Action
Show a fee breakdown before payment and preserve the same total through checkout.

## Engineering Signal
Investigate order summary recomputation and late fee insertion after seat selection.
